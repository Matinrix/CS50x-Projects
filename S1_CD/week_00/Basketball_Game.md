### 🎮 **Game Description:**

Experience a thrilling head-to-head basketball match! Two players compete to be the first to reach 10 points.

*   **Player 1 (Left):** Use the **Left Arrow** key.
*   **Player 2 (Right):** Use the **Right Arrow** key.

Simple controls, intense gameplay!
