## 🚀 **The Grand Challenge: Your Scratch Masterpiece!** 🚀

This is your time to shine! You're tasked with bringing your unique **Scratch project** to life. Whether it's a captivating story, a dynamic animation, an engaging game, or any other imaginative creation, it **must** embody the following core requirements:

---

### ✨ **Essential Ingredients for Your Project:**

1.  **Sprite Ensemble:**
    *   A minimum of **two (2) distinct sprites**.
    *   Crucially, at least one sprite **must be custom-made** (i.e., not the default Scratch Cat!).

2.  **Script Symphony:**
    *   A minimum of **three (3) scripts** are essential.
    *   These scripts can be shared across sprites or stand alone.

3.  **Logic Framework:**
    *   Integrate at least one **Conditional Statement** (like `if/then`).
    *   Implement at least one **Loop** (such as `repeat` or `forever`).
    *   Utilize at least one **Variable** to manage data within your project.

4.  **Complexity Scale:**
    *   Your project should demonstrate a level of complexity **beyond** the basic examples from class.
    *   Aim for something **more intricate** than the game `Hardest_s'lvy`.
    *   A good target is a project utilizing **several dozen code blocks**.

5.  **Custom Block Prowess:**
    *   Design and implement at least **one Custom Block** (accessible via `Block a Make`).
    *   This custom block must be capable of receiving **at least one input parameter**.
